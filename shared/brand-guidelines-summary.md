## Mission

To inspire and enable all young people, especially those who need us most, to reach their
full potential as productive, caring, responsible citizens.

---

## Brand Voice

The BGCL voice follows five pairings. Always the first, never the second.

- Assertive but not Aggressive
- Uplifting but not Unrealistic
- Expert but not Technical
- Empathetic but not Passive
- Inclusive but not Unfocused

**Overall tone:** Human, relatable, grounded, and confident. Never corporate, never
preachy, never vague. Write like a trusted champion for kids, not like a press release.

---

## Writing Rules

- Do not use em-dashes
- Do not use language that reads as generic AI writing
- Youth appear by first name only, never full name
- Never fabricate or estimate statistics. Only use figures that appear in verified
  project knowledge documents
- Avoid passive voice
- Keep sentences direct and specific

## Hashtag Standards

Every social post ends with **#GreatFuturesStartHere** and **#BGCLanier**

- Instagram: 5 to 7 hashtags total
- Facebook: 2 to 3 hashtags total
- LinkedIn: professional topical hashtags only, no branded hashtags

## Key Messages by Outcome Area

**Academic Success**
Kids need more than a safe place after school. They need support, structure, and
someone in their corner. BGCL provides tutoring, homework help, STEM programming,
and college and career readiness so every member stays on track to graduate with a
plan for the future.

**Healthy Lifestyles**
From healthy meals to physical activity to social-emotional support, BGCL meets kids
where they are and helps them build habits that carry into adulthood.

**Good Character and Citizenship**
BGCL programs build confidence, civic responsibility
